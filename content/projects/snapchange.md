snapchange is a fork of awslabs' KVM snapshot fuzzer.

The fork includes dependency updates, container and template fixes, `LD_PRELOAD` support, fuzzer virtual-memory mapping, clearer VM-exit diagnostics, and `snapshell` tooling. It is archived as a snapshot-fuzzing research reference.
